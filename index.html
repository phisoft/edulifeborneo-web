Fund-Raising

BADI ULTRA TRAIL CHALLENGE
(BUTC)

"BADI ULTRA TRAIL CHALLENGE" - Raise funds and run: 12km, 25km or 50km.

The run is in the challenging but spectacular Crocker Range Park. The start/finish is at the Tamu in Inobong. The route includes bitumen and gravel roads and lush trails through primary and secondary jungle, passing through many small villages.

Objectives of BUTC is to support programmes and activities organised by EduLife.
